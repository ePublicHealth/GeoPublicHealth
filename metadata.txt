about=GeoPublicHealth is a specialized QGIS plugin developed for epidemiologists and public health professionals. Built upon the GeoHealth Plugin by Etienne Trimaille, it integrates advanced spatial analysis methods from SIGEpi (developed by Dr. Carlos Castillo-Salgado at Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health and PAHO/WHO). Features include: spatial autocorrelation (Moran's I, LISA), disease incidence and density mapping, data blurring for privacy protection, composite index creation, and comprehensive statistical tools. Compatible with QGIS 3.42+. Optional matplotlib support for enhanced visualization. Actively maintained with regular updates.
